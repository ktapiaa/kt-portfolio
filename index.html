ABOUT ME
Born in Texas only a few years ago, I was. Moved to Indianapolis, IN in 2007 due to a family illness and never moved back. Graduated from Crispus Attucks Medical Magnet High School in '2011' and moved to Bloomington, IN shortly thereafter to study Interior Design. After a few years there, I finally moved back to Indianapolis to work for The Latino Coalition Against Domestic & Sexual Violence.
A not-for-profit organization that, as the name suggests, assists Latin@ victims of violence. I started my carrer there as a 'bilingual advocate' but shortly transitioned to a 'bilingual liason'. I had the oportunity to teach basic Microsoft classes in Spanish to the grassroot communities of Indianapolis. After a few years I met some representatives from a bank that were seeking bilingual people to work and be able to reach out to the Latino community. Seeing that my passion is to help the Latino community, I felt it was the right choice.
